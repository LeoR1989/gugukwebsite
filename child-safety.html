Child Safety Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment

GuGuk is committed to creating a safe and positive environment for all users, especially younger ones. We have zero tolerance for inappropriate behavior.

Age Restrictions

GuGuk is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Safety Features

  • Moderation: We use both automated tools and human moderators to monitor content.
  • Reporting: Users can easily report inappropriate behavior or content.
  • Privacy Controls: Users have control over who can contact them and see their profile.

Parental Guide

We encourage parents to talk to their children about online safety. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
