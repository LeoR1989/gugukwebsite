  4. Facial data. If you are a live streamer, you need to provide your facial information to complete real person verification. We will not use your facial data for any other purpose without your consent.
  5. Transaction information, such as recharge records, consumption records. We will not collect sensitive information such as your bank card number, payment password, etc. The above information will be collected by the third-party payment service provider you choose (such as Google, Apple). Please pay attention to read the online policies of such third parties.
  6. Information you provide to us by participating in activities organized by GuGuk.
  7. Other information that you agree to be obtained by us.

We recommends that you carefully consider disclosing information to other users. In addition, you should be careful not to post any confidential information about yourself, such as home address, religious affiliation or health details.

  1. If you create GuGuk account through third-party social media, such as Facebook, Google, you may need to provide your nickname, avatar and other information (depend on the third-party settings) to us.

You can also exercise your above rights by contacting us and we will respond to your request as soon as possible within a reasonable period. Please understand that in order to verify your identity, we may need you to provide relevant information.
